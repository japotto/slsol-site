Official Rules

How to Play

  • Purchase a chance/ticket according to your organizer’s instructions.
  • Each entry corresponds to a number/position on the game board.
  • At each drawing, a number is selected and the corresponding card is revealed.

Winning & Payout

  • If the Queen of Hearts is revealed, the jackpot is awarded per the official payout rules.
  • If another card is revealed, a consolation prize may be awarded and the pot carries over.

Disclaimers

Participation terms, eligibility, and payout are governed by the official rules posted by the organizers. In any conflict, the organizers’ posted rules control.
